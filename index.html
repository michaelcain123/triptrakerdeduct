TripTrakerDeduct

Simple, practical mileage & trip tracking for tax deductions.

Download for Android Download for Windows

What it does

  • Log trips with dates, mileage, vehicle, and purpose
  • Manage multiple vehicles (one active at a time)
  • Export clean CSV files for IRS / tax reporting
  • Archive, search, and bulk-delete trips
  • Early route-planning groundwork (UI + data layer)

Current status

TripTrakerDeduct is currently in beta. Real-world testing and feedback are welcome and appreciated.

  • Calendar (ICS) import is still under investigation
  • Route planning is not yet wired to live calendar data
  • iOS build is planned but not yet available
